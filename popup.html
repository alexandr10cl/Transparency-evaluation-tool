Evaluation Process: What to Expect

1. Profile Questionnaire

You'll provide basic information about your professional background. This helps contextualize your experience and insights.

2. Task Exploration

You will need to complete several tasks that will guide you through exploring the portal. These tasks will take you through the key parts of the portal so that you can evaluate it.

3. Task Review

After each completed task, you must answer a short questionnaire about your experience in solving that task.

4. Final Submission

After completing all tasks, you will answer a questionnaire to provide your overall opinion about the portal and how you felt while using it.
